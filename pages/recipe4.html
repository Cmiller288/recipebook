Swedish Meatballs Recipe

Classic comfort food with tender meatballs and creamy sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (or beef/pork mix)
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 egg
  • Salt & pepper
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Combine breadcrumbs and milk; let soak a few minutes.
  3. Mix beef, soaked crumbs, onion, egg, salt, and pepper; form meatballs.
  4. Sear meatballs in butter until browned, then transfer to a baking dish.
  5. Bake 10–12 minutes until cooked through.
  6. Meanwhile, make sauce: melt butter, add broth, simmer, stir in cream.
  7. Pour sauce over meatballs and serve. Enjoy!
