Stuffed Pork Chops Recipe

Sweet and savory pork chops stuffed to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 4 thick-cut pork chops
  • 1 package seasoned stuffing mix
  • 1/2 cup milk or water (for stuffing)
  • 1 tablespoon butter, melted
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare stuffing according to package directions using milk or water.
  3. Cut a pocket into each pork chop and stuff with prepared stuffing.
  4. Place chops in a baking dish, brush with melted butter and season.
  5. Bake 25–30 minutes until pork is cooked through and stuffing is hot.
  6. Rest 5 minutes, then serve. Enjoy!
