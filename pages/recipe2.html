Pizza Recipe

Make a tasty homemade pizza with simple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, veggies, etc.)
  • Olive oil for brushing crust

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface to desired thickness.
  3. Transfer dough to a baking sheet, brush edges with olive oil.
  4. Spread tomato sauce over dough, sprinkle cheese and toppings.
  5. Bake 10-12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  6. Slice and serve hot. Enjoy!
